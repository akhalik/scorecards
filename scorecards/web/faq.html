Frequently Asked Questions

What is Scorecards?

Scorecards is an Online Business Intelligence & Reporting Platform, aimed at Retail Sales, Distribution and Marketing Operations.

Why to choose ProductRx Scorecards for your business?

If you are inundated with large quantities of business data. And if you are looking for ways to make business decisions based on the available data. And if your current solutions are proving inadequate, then you must consider the Scorecards Platform from ProductRx. It's a powerful and yet simple and inexpensive platform which has been designed especially for sales and marketing professionals to help drive better decision making in the retail space.

Which are the key applications of the Scorecards Platform?

Scorecards is a highly customizable platform that helps drive effective decision making in the Retail Space. If your business is spread out geographically, and you need to review large quantities of data coming from these diverse areas, from various channels, concerning various product lines, and customer segments, then the Scorecards platform presents a ready solution to view such data. It helps you evaluate business performance by the various contributors. It helps you perform what if analysis to help make decisions. Finally it also helps communicate the decisions across your organization.
