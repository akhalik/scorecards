About Scorecards

Scorecards is an Online Business Intelligence & Reporting Platform, aimed at Retail Sales, Distribution and Marketing Operations.

It Helps Make Better Sense of Data by Combining Analytics with On-Ground Market Intelligence. So that you do not only know what happened, but why it happened too.

Channel Mapping - View channels by Geography, product line, etc. Get alerts and indicators based on performance, counter shares, compliance to set guidelines, etc.

Brand Visibility - Track you Ad Campaigns, Product Placements, Placement of PoS material, etc. View Stats, Charts as well as photos.
